About

Codeswains build well-crafted software to meets today's demands, while being ready for the changes tomorrow will require. We believe that in addition to making great software that just works, user privacy and data transparency are fundamental to building user relationships that last as long as our software.

“Codeswain” is an homage to the boatswains and deck mates of ships whose expertise is vital to the seafaring industries. It is their skill and foundational knowledge that keep their vessels seaworthy. Like these men and women of the sea, codeswains hone their craft and knowledge to provide expertise in the development of the software that is vital to your business.
